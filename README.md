*Core Insight*

The organization's challenge is not a visibility problem but an execution visibility problem driven by:

- Lack of individual accountability
- Low psychological safety
- Absence of structured daily planning

Technology currently reports symptoms but does not influence execution behavior.


*Final Insight Paragraph*

The three situations appear independent but are manifestations of the same underlying organizational condition. 
Employees do not experience the current system as a mechanism for support and problem solving. 
They experience it as a mechanism for reporting and scrutiny. 
Until accountability becomes individualized, planning becomes structured, and transparency becomes psychologically safe, additional dashboards and automation will increase reporting volume without improving execution quality. 
The objective is therefore not to digitize the current behavior but to redesign the behavior first and use technology to reinforce it.
